My Swachta App- recycle, reusable and decompose

  • Society Registration By Admin

    Society is register by an admin. Users of the respective society can register and upload wastes, and classify it to determine whether it is recyclable or reusable.

  • Classification By CNN Model

    Once the waste is classify and uploaded by the user, they can accordingly donate it to nearby ngo's, slum area's and if it is decomposable the book schedule to pick up wastes.

  • Transition to Full Service

    Track the no of wastes uploded by the users and after every 7 days per society user will get rewards.

  • Collect Rewards

    Finally User can collect their rewards and waste management problem solved and it is reuse,recycle and dispose.
